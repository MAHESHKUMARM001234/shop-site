What is online shopping ?

Online shopping or e-shopping is searching for and purchasing goods and services over the internet through use of a web browser.

why the online shopping is best?

Customer can purchase items from the comfort of their own home or workspace. Shopping is made easier and convenient for the customer through the internet. It is also easy to cancel the transactions. Save the time and efforts.

How the customers are earn the online shopping ?

Cashback and Rewards Programs: Many online retailers offer cashback or rewards programs, where customers can earn a percentage of their purchase value as cashback or points. These rewards can be redeemed for discounts or future purchases. Referral Programs: Some online stores have referral programs where customers can earn credits or discounts by referring friends or family members to the website or app. Loyalty Programs: Online retailers may have loyalty programs that reward repeat customers with exclusive discounts, early access to sales, or special offers.
